Aquarium Guide

How to Feed Fishes at Home

Feed your fish 1–2 times daily using high-quality food specific to their species. Avoid overfeeding—only give as much as they can consume in 2–3 minutes to prevent water contamination.

Types of Home Pet Fishes

Common pet fishes include Goldfish, Guppies, Betta Fish, Tetras, Mollies, Swordtails, and Angelfish. Choose species based on water type, tank size, and compatibility.

Types of Aquariums

  • Freshwater Aquariums – Ideal for beginners, easy to maintain.
  • Saltwater Aquariums – For colorful marine fish and corals, requires advanced care.
  • Brackish Aquariums – Mix of fresh and salt water, for species like puffers and gobies.
  • Planted Tanks – Freshwater tanks with aquatic plants for natural aquascaping.
  • Biotope Aquariums – Mimic a specific habitat (Amazon river, African lakes).

Sizes of Aquariums

  • Small Tanks (5–10 gallons): Good for Betta or small community fish.
  • Medium Tanks (10–30 gallons): Perfect for beginners with more space for fish.
  • Large Tanks (30–75 gallons): Suitable for diverse species and aquascaping.
  • Extra Large (75+ gallons): Best for saltwater setups or large species.
